About Us

Our mission is to equip students with the fundamental knowledge of computer science and industry-required stacks grounded with strong moral values, and to seamlessly integrate them into the workforce as skilled professionals.

At Ustadam, Our five year vision includes

  • Trainings:

    Prepare a work force of 100,000 skilled workers who can earn their livelihood through the paid and unpaid training courses and workshops.

  • Tech Startups:

    Partner with/Produce 10 Technological startups.

  • Products:

    Develop 5 IT products that help individuals and organizations improve their efficiency.

  • Schools as Partners:

    Partner with five schools to train teachers and students in technology and life skills.

  • Place of Business:

    Set up a marketplace that exports IT workforce and IT products to international markets.
